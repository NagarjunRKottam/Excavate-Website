Our Story

A Sports Bar. A Lounge. One Address.

We opened Excavate because we were tired of picking. If you wanted sports, you got bright lights and cheap beer. If you wanted a lounge, you got deep seats but no game. We wanted both — the roar of the final quarter and the low hum of a room that knows how to slow down.

So we built it. Twenty screens. Forty flavors. One unmistakable vibe.

The name comes from the idea that the best nights are the ones you dig into — the stories you uncover with your crew over wings, cocktails, and a long hookah session. That’s what we’re here for.
